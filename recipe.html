Macaroni Recipe

Ingredients

  1. Macaroni
  2. Chicken Breast
  3. Frozen Vegetables
  4. Mixed Spice
  5. Black and Red Pepper
  6. Cooking Oil
  7. Finely chopped red onions
  8. Finely chopped fresh coriander
  9. Tomato paste
  10. Bell peppers

Instructions

  • Boil the chicken breast in water until fully cooked and set it aside to cool.
  • While the chicken cools, chop your tomatoes, bell peppers, and mince the ginger. Set them aside.
  • Once the chicken has cooled, slice it into smaller pieces or shred it into strings.
  • Heat some cooking oil in a pan and saut√© the onions until golden brown.
  • Stir in the minced ginger, red and black pepper, mixed spice, and turmeric. Cook for a minute to release the flavors.
  • Add the chopped tomatoes and frozen vegetables to the pan. Cook until soft and well combined.
  • Stir in the cooked chicken and tomato paste. Allow it to cook for a few more minutes.
  • Combine the mixture with cooked macaroni, garnish with fresh coriander, and serve.

Amount of Ingredients

Item Amount
Macaroni 1.5 kg
Chicken breast 1 piece
Frozen vegetables 1 kg
Bell peppers 4
Red onion 1
Mixed spice 1 tablespoon
